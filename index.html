Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries from parents.

How does the camp address AI's impact on human artists and copyright?

We center the rights of creators. During our Image Forge day, we critically examine how AI models are trained and discuss the importance of consent, copyright, and fair compensation for artists.

Does the program cover the environmental impact of AI?

Yes. Training and running AI requires data centers that consume significant amounts of energy and water. We discuss this ecological cost, teaching students about the carbon footprint of digital tools and the need for climate-conscious technology.

How do you handle algorithmic bias and the ethical challenges of AI?

We believe critical education is essential. We aren't teaching kids to blindly consume AI; we equip them with the digital literacy to spot algorithmic bias, understand data privacy, and ask important questions about how these technologies affect society.

What age group is this camp for?

BrAIn Camp is specifically designed for students ages 8 to 11. However, if you believe your child would be a great fit outside of this range (such as an older/younger sibling or a gifted student), please reach out to us at brAInCampWA@gmail.com to discuss!

Does my child need to bring a phone or laptop?

No! Instruction is delivered using secure, program-provided smartphones. All necessary technology is included in the tuition fee.

How do you ensure screen safety?

Safety is our top priority. Students interact with a customized AI model in a completely controlled environment. Our provided devices enforce strict behavioral and content filters to ensure students won't access anything inappropriate.

What are the daily hours?

Our full-day sessions run Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

We believe every child should have the opportunity to develop digital literacy. If you are struggling financially, please reach out to us at brAInCampWA@gmail.com to discuss scholarship options and partial tuition waivers.

What should my camper bring?

Please pack a daily snack, a water bottle, and a lunch for your child. All tech tools and camp materials are provided.

Who do I contact if I have more questions?

You can reach out to us anytime at brAInCampWA@gmail.com.
